Our fee structures are transparent and based on the complexity of the case. We primarily work on a fixed-fee basis for standard litigation, providing our clients with financial predictability. For corporate consultancy and high-value arbitration, we may utilize an hourly rate or a retainer model. A detailed fee breakdown is provided following the initial consultation.
To schedule an appointment, you may use our online booking system or contact our office directly via telephone. Initial consultations are scheduled within 24-48 hours. During this session, we conduct a preliminary case review to determine viability and required legal resources.
Advocate Muhammad Ahmed specializes in Corporate Law, Civil Litigation, Family Law, and Property Disputes. We also provide robust representation in Criminal Defense and Tax matters. Our firm is equipped to handle both individual legal needs and large-scale corporate legal frameworks.
We represent clients across all levels of the Pakistani judiciary, including District Courts, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of Pakistan. Our expertise extends to specialized tribunals such as the Banking Court, Labor Court, and the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) appeals.
Our office is open Monday through Saturday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (PST). Emergency legal assistance is available for existing clients through a dedicated 24/7 hotline provided upon retainer agreement.
Clients are requested to bring a valid CNIC, any prior court orders, relevant contracts, and a chronological summary of the events pertaining to their case. For property matters, original title deeds or certified copies are essential for the preliminary review.
